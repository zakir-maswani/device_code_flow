Now lets talk about Cross validation ham sikhengy k CV kis terh validation data ko use krta hai 
CV ka sab sy popular example jo hota hai wo hai k-fold cross validation. CV ki multiple types hn jis mn k-fold cross validation ek most popular type hai

Lets suppose data k andr we have 1000 values  
sab sy pahly ham train aur test sets mn split krengy 
80% training kliey
20% testing kliye

Means model ki training kliye hamary pass 800 values hn aur testing kliye 200 values

Ab issi 800 values k uppr cross validation apply krengy .
K-fold cross validation k andr ham apny data ko k-folds ky andr divide krty hn, k folds means k parts k andr divide krty hn jsy for example, yh hamara data hai 
ham ny k ki value lay li hai k= 5 iska matlab hai ham apny data ko 5 parts k andr divide kr rhy hn yh value 6, 10 11 kuch bhi ho skti hai. Filhal kliye hamara data 5 euqal folds (parts) mn divided hoga.
Harr individual part fold kahlayga fold-1, fold-2, fold-3, fold-4, and fold-5


To k-fold cross validation k andr ham kia krengy sab sy pahly kisi ek fold ko as a validation data k toor py use kr k baqi k 4 folds py model train krengy yh hogi hamari iteration 1 aur k-fold cv k andr ham k iterations krty hn 
taky koe na koe ek fold hamary validation data k toor py lazmi select ho jay 

Iteration one k andr lets suppose hamara model prepare ho gia aur hamary model ki kuch na kuch accuracy nikla k aai 
Iss same cheez ko ham k times repeat krengy 
Dosry iteration mn fold-2 as a validation data kam kry ga aur baqi k folds (fold1, fold3, fold4, and fold-5) k uppr model train hoga aur kuch na kuch accuracy aai gi  

Iss ka benefit yh h k end the end suppose kren ham ny 5 iteration ki hain, in iterations ki jitni bhi accuracies hn un ka average calculate kr final accuracy find krengy 
Means final accuracy un sab accuracies ki average hogi, iss case mn hamara model ziayda reliable hoga as comapre to simple train_test_split k yahi benefit hota hai Cross Validation ka, model ki performance ziyada stable hogi

Kabhi kabhi asa hota hai k test data kuch biased ho jata hai  means koe class 80% exist krti ho, kabhi kabhi asa ho skta hai k model test data k uppr achy results data ho, jab bhi ham cross validation apply krty hn hamary pass ek tariky sy average performance aati hai jis ki wajah sy biasness aur zidaya kam ho jati hai 

Aur agr hamary modek k andr koe bhi hyperparamter nh hai fir bhi use kr skty hn,  cross validation ka one of the most imprtant use cases hai Hyperparameter tuning.
Means kabhi bhi agr hamara alpha jsa, ya lambda jsa, ya k jsa agr koe hyperparameter ki ham ny best values figure out krni hai to wahan pr ham hyperparamter tuning kliye ham Cross validation ka use krty hn.
